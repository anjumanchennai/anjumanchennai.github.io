Masjid Expansion Projects

Assalamu Alaikkum,

We are requesting your support for an important project that is dear to the hearts of our community. We are proposing to renovate our mosque.

The proposed Phase - 1 construction is located at Plot No. 35 to 39, Rangadoss Reddy Colony, Villivakkam and will have a capacity of 550 people for the congregational prayers in 3430 sq.ft. The estimated cost of the construction is Rs.80 Lacs.

Insha Allah, we believe that this mosque will serve as a vital center of community life, providing a place for prayer, education, and socialization. We have consulted the local authorities and have the support of the community members who have recognized the need of the mosque.

We are reaching out to individuals and organizations like yourself to ask for your support in making this project a reality. We need your help in the form of donations, materials, and volunteer work.

We would be grateful for any support you can offer. If you would like to contribute, please contact us at [Phone No: 9043744991] or visit our mosque.

Thank you for your consideration and support. Jazakallah khair.
